Open NTP – 51 (avg score)

NTP is the Network Time Protocol, a service on the Internet which keeps computers' clocks in sync to a millisecond precision. For historic reasons, there are many open NTP servers on the Internet.

Since NTP runs over UDP, it is also vulnerable to reflective UDP amplification attacks.

Details

NTP amplification attacks have a considerable amplification factor: up to 550. An attacker with 1 MBit/sec connections can find 10 open ntp servers, each with a GBit/sec connection and thus create a DDOS attack of 500 GBit/sec.
