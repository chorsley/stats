Open SSDP

SSDP - the Simple Service Discovery Protocol is used for advertisement and discovery of network services and presence information. It is often enabled in customer end devices (CPEs) such as modems, WLAN modems. Since SSDP runs over UDP, again, it can be used for UDP reflection attacks.

Details

SSDP's amplification factor is ~ 30. It is less powerful than NTP.
